The beginnings of Tesla

Tesla was founded in 2003 by a group of engineers, led by
entrepreneur and visionary Elon Musk.

The company focused from its inception on
the production of high-end electric vehicles,
with a focus on battery technology and energy
efficiency.

During its early years, Tesla faced many challenges and obstacles
on its path to success, having to deal with financial problems,
criticism from the established automotive industry, and
technological challenges in developing high-performance batteries
and affordable electric vehicles.
